About Me

I am a senior researcher in the Department of Data Science & System Security at NEC Laboratories, America.

I have worked in the areas of machine learning, data mining, and data management. My recent research work focuses on graph representation learning, natural language understanding, density estimation for complex data, and imitation learning. My work has provided solutions to real-life problems in critical domains, including cybersecurity, complex system management, finance, software engineering, and healthcare.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science at University of California, Santa Barbara.
